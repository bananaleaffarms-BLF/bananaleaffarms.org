Our Apiary

Beekeeping Programs & Classes

Banana Leaf Farms maintains three registered colonies in Vallejo. We offer hands-on beekeeping education from beginner hive dives to advanced swarm management — rooted in real, working apiary experience.

🍯

Apitherapy Hive Dive

A hands-on inspection discovering all 7 medicinal bee products where they naturally occur — pollen, propolis, beeswax, royal jelly, bee bread, bee venom, and honey. Includes a honey tasting card and take-home reference samples.

🐝

Swarm-Style Hive Splits

Learn how to move the laying queen to a new hive, allow the parent colony to raise a new queen naturally, and use splits as a powerful mite management tool. Includes a split-day checklist and post-split decision guide.

🌻

Beginner Beekeeping

Introduction to hive structure, bee biology, seasonal management, equipment, and safety. Perfect for anyone curious about keeping bees at home or in their community garden.

📋

Mite Management & Winter Prep

Evidence-based Varroa mite treatment protocols, winterization checklists, and colony health monitoring. Taught from real BLF apiary experience including first-year mite treatments and winter close-out.

Food Entrepreneurship

MEHKO: Microenterprise Home Kitchen Operations

🍳 Our MEHKO Journey

A Microenterprise Home Kitchen Operation (MEHKO) allows preparing and selling homemade meals legally from your home kitchen — empowering local food entrepreneurs and celebrating cultural food traditions.

We're launching our own MEHKO focused on plant-based, nourishing, community meals. Our founder is a Cook Academy Cohort 7 graduate with MEHKO certification in progress.

Contact Us About Our MEHKO

Start Your Own MEHKO

Learn the Rules

Review the California MEHKO Overview (CDPH) to understand requirements and eligible foods.
