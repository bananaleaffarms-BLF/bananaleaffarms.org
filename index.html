Who We Are

Growing Community with Sustainable Agriculture

We are dedicated to educating the community about sustainable agriculture and horticulture. Through workshops and events, we focus on organic farming, beekeeping, and environmental stewardship to enhance local food security and foster community well-being.

Our vision: a Vallejo where every family has the knowledge, skills, and community to grow, preserve, and share healthy food.

Our Apiary

Beekeeping Programs & Classes

Three registered colonies in Vallejo. Hands-on education from beginner hive dives to advanced swarm management.

🍯

Apitherapy Hive Dive

Hands-on inspection of all 7 medicinal bee products — pollen, propolis, beeswax, royal jelly, bee bread, bee venom, and honey. Includes honey tasting.

🐝

Swarm-Style Hive Splits

Move the laying queen, allow natural queen rearing, and use splits as a powerful mite management tool. Includes split-day checklist.

🌻

Beginner Beekeeping

Hive structure, bee biology, seasonal management, equipment, and safety. Perfect for anyone curious about backyard beekeeping.

📋

Mite Management & Winter Prep

Evidence-based Varroa protocols, winterization, and colony health monitoring from real BLF apiary experience.

Food Entrepreneurship

MEHKO: Microenterprise Home Kitchen Operations

🍳 Our MEHKO Journey

A MEHKO lets you prepare and sell homemade meals legally from your home kitchen — empowering local food entrepreneurs and celebrating cultural food traditions.

We're launching our own MEHKO focused on plant-based, nourishing meals. Our founder is a Cook Academy Cohort 7 graduate with MEHKO certification in progress.

Nonprofit Consulting Services

Supportive, hands-on consulting for emerging nonprofits. We've built our own foundation from the ground up — and we're here to help you do the same.

Articles of incorporation, EIN setup, 501(c)(3) application (Form 1023-EZ), California charity registration, city business licensing, and ongoing compliance guidance. We've completed every step ourselves.
Facilitated development of your organizational identity — mission statement crafting, values alignment, and the strategic clarity needed to communicate your purpose to funders and community alike.
Implementation of Zoho Books for nonprofits — chart of accounts, invoice templates, P&L setup, bank reconciliation, and quarterly financial reporting workflows.
Grant research, SAM.gov registration, DUNS/UEI setup, application support, budget narrative writing, and post-award reporting — from government grants to private foundations.
Website development (HTML/GitHub Pages), Google for Nonprofits setup, Canva Nonprofit access, Google Analytics, social media strategy, and newsletter systems.
Connecting organizations with local resources, cooperative memberships, government programs, SCORE mentorship, and community networks specific to your mission area.
Bylaws drafting, board meeting minutes templates, officer structure, conflict of interest policies, and strategies for building toward a strong, diverse board of directors.
Logic model development, KPI frameworks, program documentation, impact measurement tools, and annual report structure — helping you prove and communicate your impact.
Ongoing 1:1 support and mentorship for nonprofit founders navigating the early stages of organizational development. Sliding scale pricing to support mission-aligned work.

Every service is rooted in lived experience — we've completed each step ourselves. Offered on a sliding scale to support mission-aligned organizations.
